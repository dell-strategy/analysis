Executive Summary & Macro Landscape

This section provides a high-level overview of NYC's political climate and core administrative goals. Aligning infrastructure pitches (like APEX or PowerEdge) with the Mayor's public-facing promises is critical.

🏙️

Political Climate

Under Mayor Eric Adams, the administration focuses on operational efficiency, public safety, and leveraging tech to solve systemic issues. There is immense pressure to consolidate disjointed IT systems across 100+ agencies.

📈

Economic Climate

Facing budget constraints from migrant crises and post-pandemic commercial real estate lulls, NYC is heavily scrutinizing net-new IT spend. ROI and "do more with less" (cloud cost optimization, storage efficiency) are paramount.

🎯

Top FY Goals

  • Public Safety & Cyber: Centralizing threat intelligence.
  • Citizen Experience: Expanding "MyCity" single sign-on portal.
  • Digital Equity & AI: Ethical AI deployment and broadband.

IT Governance & Service Delivery Model

Operating Model: Moving to Consolidated

Historically federated, the creation of the Office of Technology and Innovation (OTI) unified DoITT, Cyber Command, and 311. However, massive agencies (NYPD, DOE) still maintain shadow IT and independent infrastructure.

Friction Points

Agencies resist OTI mandate due to chargeback models and perceived slowness. Opportunity: Dell can position APEX custom cloud to give OTI central billing control while giving agencies self-service agility.

Governance Transformation
Federated (Legacy) ➜ Consolidated (OTI)
