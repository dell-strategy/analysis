IT Ops & Procurement

Navigating the Envision 2026 centralized governance model, CDT chargebacks, and crucial compliance drivers.

⚖

IT Governance Model (Envision 2026)

Forced Centralization. Historically federated, agencies are now corralled by CDT's Envision 2026 framework and the mandatory Project Approval Lifecycle (PAL). CalCloud was sunset in Dec 2023, forcing a shift to the California Managed Cloud (CAMC) or hybrid architectures.

Severe Financial Friction Points (The Dell Opportunity)

  • 4% TCRI: CDT implemented a 4% Targeted Cost Recovery Increase on services.
  • CAMC Markup: 17% markup on top of public cloud vendor pass-through costs.
  • Non-Standard Penalty: Agencies running legacy gear face a 20% (Yr 1) up to 50% (Yr 3) monthly surcharge per instance.

Agencies are squeezed by a 7.95% budget cut while CDT raises rates. Pitch APEX on-prem as the cost-predictable bypass to central administrative bloat.

✔ Key Compliance Drivers

  • CCPA & Delete Act (2026): Requires Drop platforms and strict 2028 cyber audits. Demands immutable storage snapshots.
  • Cal-Secure: Mandates Zero Trust architecture (SIMM 5355-B) across all state endpoints.
  • StateRAMP & Cloud Smart: Vendors must pass the rigorous CCSA assessment.
  • AI Transparency Act (AB 853): Operative Aug 2026, requires provenance data tracking.

Primary Procurement Vehicles

  • CMAS: Heavily used for commercial off-the-shelf (COTS) IT goods to bypass competitive bidding.
  • SLP (Software Licensing Program): Preferred for enterprise software/SaaS.
  • NASPO ValuePoint & OMNIA: Flexible mechanisms for complex cloud/hardware via resellers like SHI.
  • Challenge-Based: Envision 2026 shifts to requiring working PoCs in a sandbox prior to award.

The Battlefield (Competitors)

  • Mainframe Ecosystem: Massive legacy footprint of IBM z15/z16 and AIX Power10. Being actively deprecated for x86 RedHat.
  • Storage Contenders: NetApp/IBM legacy SANs are under fire from Pure Storage, Vast Data, and Nexsan pushing high-density NVMe.
  • Public Cloud: AWS/Azure facing repatriation backlash due to unpredictable costs and the 17% CAMC markup.

Incumbent Disruption Tactics

  • Pure / Vast Data Takedown: Directly leverage that Dell PowerScale uses up to 72% less energy and 80% less rack space to run the same Nvidia AI infrastructure. Exceeds state sustainability goals.
  • Legacy Mainframe Shift: Capitalize on the punitive 50% non-standard surcharges by migrating IBM workloads to high-density Dell x86.
