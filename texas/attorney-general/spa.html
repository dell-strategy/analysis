The "Federal Funds" Hook

Over 60% of the OAG's budget comes from Federal Funds, primarily for Child Support enforcement.

Sales Implication: Federal money comes with federal strings. Compliance (IRS Pub 1075, NIST 800-53) is a massive driver. If Dell solutions can automate or simplify compliance reporting, it's a winning argument.

IT Spending Power

Because the Child Support Division (CSD) generates revenue (collections), they have more flexibility and larger capital project budgets than the purely "taxpayer funded" divisions like Legal or Law Enforcement.

Target: Focus large-scale infrastructure and modernization pitches on CSD leadership.

Organizational Landscape

The OAG is functionally split. The "Law Firm" side (Legal Divisions) is prestigious but small. The "Operations" side (Child Support) is an enterprise-scale logistics machine.
