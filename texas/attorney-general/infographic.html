Executive Summary

The Texas Office of the Attorney General (OAG) acts as the legal counselor and representative for the state. Unlike typical law firms, the OAG is a massive operational engine, primarily driven by the Child Support Division (CSD), which accounts for the vast majority of its budget and personnel. For Dell, the strategic opportunity lies in IT modernization, cybersecurity resilience, and data center optimization to support this massive case-management workload.
