Overview

The Florida Department of Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles (FLHSMV) is the state agency responsible for driver licensing and identification, motor-vehicle and vessel titling and registration, and the Florida Highway Patrol (FHP). It operates statewide, high-transaction systems that touch nearly every Florida resident — the customer-facing MyDMV Portal for license and registration services, the credentialing systems behind physical and (relaunching) mobile IDs, and the public-safety IT that supports troopers in the field. The agency works through a network of county tax collectors and license-plate agents who deliver many in-person services, which makes data integration, identity verification, and secure service delivery central to its technology footprint. For a Dell SLG seller, FLHSMV is an enterprise-modernization, digital-identity, and law-enforcement-technology account rather than a single-program buyer: its Office of Motorist Modernization (OMM) is explicitly chartered to replace antiquated systems, and its public-safety mission drives demand for compute, storage, security, and edge/field infrastructure.

Initiatives & Priorities

Motorist Modernization (MyDMV Portal)

FLHSMV's Office of Motorist Modernization is chartered to replace antiquated systems with flexible architecture, improve service delivery, and strengthen data quality and security. The customer-facing MyDMV Portal digitizes driver-license, registration, title, and parking-permit transactions — a multi-year enterprise-modernization track touching identity, data, and cloud.

Modernization Digital Services
Mobile driver's license relaunch

After pulling the Thales-built 'Florida Smart ID' from app stores in 2024, Florida is relaunching its mDL. HB 543 (passed the House March 6, 2026) would require ISO/IEC 18013-5 and 18013-7 compliance, selective disclosure, no state tracking, and unlinkable transactions. A high-profile digital-identity and credentialing modernization to track closely.

Digital ID Security
Identity verification / fraud prevention

FLHSMV has moved to modernize identity-proofing for online services (reporting cited an upgrade toward selfie-biometric and identity-database verification for the MyDMV Portal). Identity assurance, fraud prevention, and secure authentication are recurring needs as more transactions shift online.

Identity Cybersecurity
Public-safety & field technology (FHP)

Florida Highway Patrol drives demand for in-vehicle and field technology — law-enforcement software, crash-diagramming tools, radio/communications, and an FY2026-27 emphasis on aviation and fleet. A path for edge compute, ruggedized devices, storage, and secure connectivity.
