Overview

The Arizona Department of Administration (ADOA) is the state's central support agency, and two of its offices matter most to an IT seller: the Arizona Strategic Enterprise Technology (ASET) office and the State Procurement Office (SPO). ASET is Arizona's statewide IT authority — it sets technology strategy, policy and standards, delivers shared enterprise services, and oversees high-risk technology projects across state agencies through the IT Authorization Committee (ITAC) and the Project Investment Justification (PIJ) process. State CIO J.R. Sloan leads ASET and is the top relationship for the statewide motion; he is best known for Arizona's Cloud-First Policy, which has driven the closure of roughly 90 on-premise data centers and migration of over 2,900 applications. SPO, also under ADOA, runs the statewide contracts and the Arizona State Purchasing Cooperative that local governments, schools and nonprofits buy through. ADOA is more conduit and oversight body than large end-spender: the biggest agency IT budgets sit with the agencies themselves, but ADOA shapes the policy, the vehicles, and the project approvals those buys flow through.

Budget & Fiscal

ADOA-ASET's leverage is policy, oversight and procurement rather than a large discretionary IT appropriation. ASET reviews and oversees agency technology projects statewide via ITAC/PIJ, while the dollars for most projects sit in the sponsoring agencies' budgets. Initiatives & Priorities

Cloud-First consolidation

Arizona's long-running Cloud-First Policy (closing on-premise data centers, migrating apps to cloud and SaaS) remains a core ASET program. Position infrastructure, hybrid-cloud and migration offerings around the next wave of workloads.

Cloud Modernization
Generative AI policy & adoption

ASET maintains a statewide Generative AI policy (P2000), the state has rolled out employee Gen AI training, and Governor Hobbs stood up an AI Steering Committee. AI governance, data readiness and responsible adoption are active priorities — tie AI-infrastructure plays to the policy guardrails.

AI Policy
Statewide data strategy

Arizona's CIO, CISO and chief data officer are jointly driving a statewide data strategy emphasizing data governance and readiness — a foundation for AI and analytics workloads.

Data Strategy
Statewide cybersecurity & cyber readiness

ASET sets statewide security policy and standards; a Statewide Cyber Readiness program extends resources to local and tribal governments. Security and Zero Trust modernization run alongside the cloud and AI tracks.
