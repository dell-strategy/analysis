Charlotte is experiencing explosive population growth and aggressive corporate recruitment. The city is forced to modernize legacy systems rapidly to accommodate an expanding tax base and complex data telemetry.

Sales Context: The administration heavily favors capitalized IT modernization, bond-funded infrastructure, and ROI-driven efficiencies due to a zero-tax-increase mandate for FY26. Operational expenses (OpEx) are highly constrained, meaning major hardware deployments must be structured as multi-year capital investments or consumption-based models (like APEX).

Chargeback Friction

The city utilizes complex Internal Service Funds (ISF). Central I&T charges departments based on usage metrics. Large enterprise projects (like Workday) force departments to surrender operational budgets.

Sales Warning: Central I&T sales must consider end-user agency backlash against non-transparent chargebacks.
