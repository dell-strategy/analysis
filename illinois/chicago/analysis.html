🏛️ The Current Climate & Priorities

Under the current mayoral administration, Chicago is navigating a projected ~$982M structural budget deficit while attempting to execute a progressive agenda heavily focused on neighborhood equity and public safety reform. The core challenge for the IT organization (BIA) is balancing required legacy infrastructure modernization against these high-visibility digital initiatives within a constrained General Corporate Fund.

Pitch Angle: Technology must not be positioned as overhead. Every Dell APEX or PowerStore pitch must map to "Operational Cost-Reduction" or compliance with federal mandates (like the CPD Consent Decree).

🚓

✈️

🎯 Named Initiatives

Map Dell's portfolio directly to these funded municipal projects.

O'Hare 21 & Terminal Modernization (CDA)

A historic $8.5B transformation of O'Hare's terminal footprint. Beyond concrete, it requires a massive overhaul of the IT backbone to support biometric boarding, automated baggage tracking, and seamless passenger Wi-Fi experiences.

Dell Solution Mapping
  • Dell NativeEdge & VxRail: Centralized management platform for distributed IoT edge devices across miles of new terminal space, ensuring 99.999% uptime for localized compute.
  • PowerStore: High-performance, low-latency storage required for the massive databases underpinning real-time flight operations and passenger processing.

Federal Consent Decree Data Compliance (CPD)

The Chicago Police Department is under a federal mandate to improve transparency. This necessitates a massive expansion of high-definition body-cam and dash-cam data storage, with strict chain-of-custody and CJIS requirements.

Dell Solution Mapping
  • Dell PowerScale (Isilon): The industry standard for unstructured video storage. Easily scales to petabytes to handle video retention requirements without breaking compliance.
  • PowerEdge XE (AI-Ready): GPU-accelerated servers specifically designed to run on-prem AI workloads (like video redaction) without sending sensitive data to the public cloud.

Cloud Repatriation & "Cloud Smart"

BIA is experiencing unexpected hyper-scaler egress fees and complex management. They are initiating a strategy to pull predictable, steady-state workloads back on-premise to save the general fund money.

Dell Solution Mapping
  • Dell APEX Custom Solutions: Pitch the "Cloud Experience On-Prem." BIA gets predictable OpEx billing, elasticity, and self-service, while eliminating hyperscaler egress fees.

Municipal Ransomware Resilience (SLCGP)

Escalating municipal ransomware threats have forced the city to leverage State and Local Cybersecurity Grant Program (SLCGP) funds to mandate immutable backups across all departments.

Dell Solution Mapping
  • Dell PowerProtect Cyber Recovery: An isolated, air-gapped data vault. Essential pitch for SLCGP cybersecurity grant funding.
  • CyberSense: AI-driven analytics that scan the backup vault to detect corruption and ensure the city has a clean copy of data to recover from.

"Chicago Connected" & Digital Equity

A massive mayoral push to expand high-speed broadband access and provide devices to students in historically underserved South and West side wards to close the digital divide.

Dell Solution Mapping
  • Client Solutions Group (CSG): Bulk procurement of Chromebooks and Latitude devices.
  • Dell PCaaS: PC as a Service to help the city manage device lifecycle and refresh schedules without large capital outlays.

Smart City Intersection & Traffic Expansion

Deployment of connected sensors across intersections for real-time traffic management, gunshot detection integration, and environmental monitoring.

Dell Solution Mapping
  • Dell Edge Gateways: Ruggedized, temperature-resistant compute nodes that can be mounted at traffic intersections to process data locally.
