IT Governance & Service Delivery

Transitioning from a federated model to a strict Consolidated/Shared Services model.

Cloud-First Mandate

BCIT is dismantling agency fiefdoms to establish enterprise standards. Driving a "cloud-first, cloud-only" posture to virtualize workloads out of vulnerable closets into centralized/SaaS platforms.

EPMO Established:

Enterprise Portfolio Management Office now acts as the gatekeeper, eliminating redundant software and enforcing cyber standards.

Major Friction Points

  • ISF Chargebacks

    Agencies resent BCIT billing them for shared resources (storage, network).

    Dell Play: Pitch TCO reduction (VxRail) to lower ISF rates.
  • OIG Oversight

    High-profile IT failures ($4.8M website fiasco) lead to extreme risk aversion and elongated sales cycles.

  • Zero Trust Whiplash

    A $1.5M Workday EFT fraud forced abrupt Zero Trust mandates, causing inter-agency workflow friction.

Overall City Budget Overview (FY 2026)

The overarching $4.63 Billion adopted budget is heavily skewed towards operational expenditures (keeping the city running), while the Capital Improvement Program (CIP) dictates long-term infrastructure and technology buys.

Sales Strategy: Frame Dell hardware purchases not just as one-off capital buys, but as critical enablers that reduce long-term operational costs (O&M) within the larger budget.

Procurement & Compliance

Navigating purchasing vehicles and rigid compliance mandates.

Preferred Procurement Vehicles

  • State of MD Master Contracts CATS+ for professional services. BPO 060B2490022 for Hardware. Most critical avenue via PORFP.
  • National & Regional Co-ops OMNIA, NASPO ValuePoint (heavily used for client/endpoints). BRCPC for local municipal piggybacking.

Vendor Battlefield

Dell Technologies: Formidable tier-one footprint. PowerMax (CJIS/MAFIS), Unity, VxRail, Latitude Chromebooks.

HPE: Aggressive rip-and-replace campaigns nearby (Harford Co. displaced VxRail with vSAN nodes).

Nutanix: Emerging HCI threat on state master contracts.

Cisco & Motorola: Dominant in core networking and 800 MHz radio respectively.

Regulatory & Compliance Drivers

Zero Trust & IAM

After a $1.5M EFT fraud in Workday, state mandates strict Zero Trust Access within 18 months. Continuous MFA and StateRAMP equivalent required.

CJIS Compliance

For upcoming CAD replacement ($11.5M). Any compute/storage touching law enforcement must meet FBI CJIS standards (end-to-end encryption).

AI Governance

MD AI Subcabinet requires algorithmic transparency, bias mitigation, and strict data sovereignty. Public LLMs are heavily restricted for civic data.
