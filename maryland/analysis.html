IT Operating Model

Hybrid Structure

Maryland operates under a hybrid model. DoIT provides centralized enterprise infrastructure (network, central data center, core security). However, massive agencies (e.g., MDOT, MDH) still maintain significant federated application environments.

⚠️ The APEX Opportunity (Friction Points)

DoIT utilizes a chargeback model for enterprise services. High chargeback rates frequently cause friction. Large federated agencies attempt to build "shadow IT" or experience bill shock from hyperscaler egress fees.

Strategy: Position Dell APEX as the localized, OPEX alternative to bridge this gap, offering cloud-like consumption without public cloud egress fees or DoIT overhead.
